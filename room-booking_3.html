Terms & Conditions

Please read and agree before proceeding to payment

1. Booking & Confirmation

Your booking is not confirmed until full payment has been received. A booking confirmation with reference number will be sent to your email address. Toynbee Studios reserves the right to decline any booking at its discretion.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 14 days before the booking date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made 7–14 days in advance will receive a 50% refund. No refund will be given for cancellations made less than 7 days before the booking date. All cancellations must be confirmed in writing to spaces@toynbeestudios.org.uk.

3. Use of Space

Hirers are responsible for leaving the space in the condition in which it was found. Any damage to equipment, furniture or the building will be charged to the hirer. Spaces must be vacated promptly at the end of the hired period. Noise levels must not disturb other users of the building.

4. Capacity & Health and Safety

Hirers must not exceed the stated capacity of the space at any time. All hirers are responsible for ensuring the safety of their participants and must comply with fire safety procedures displayed in each space.

5. Equipment & Technical

Basic technical equipment is included in the hire fee where indicated. Additional technical support must be arranged in advance and may incur additional charges. Hirers bring their own equipment at their own risk.

6. Catering & Refreshments

Hirers may bring their own refreshments but must remove all food waste at the end of the hire period. Hot food preparation is not permitted unless the kitchen has been booked separately.

7. Liability

Toynbee Studios / Artsadmin accepts no liability for loss or damage to property belonging to the hirer or their guests. Hirers are strongly advised to arrange their own public liability insurance.

8. Data Protection

Your personal data will be held in accordance with our Privacy Policy and used solely for the purpose of administering your booking. We will not share your data with third parties except as required by law.
