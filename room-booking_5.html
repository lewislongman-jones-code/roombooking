Terms & Conditions

Please read and agree before proceeding to payment

1. Booking & Confirmation

Your booking is not confirmed until full payment has been received. A confirmation with your booking reference will be sent to your email address. Toynbee Studios reserves the right to decline any booking at its discretion.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 14 days before the booking date receive a full refund. Cancellations 7–14 days in advance receive a 50% refund. No refund is given for cancellations within 7 days of the booking. All cancellations must be submitted in writing to spaces@toynbeestudios.org.uk.

3. Use of Space

Hirers are responsible for leaving the space in the condition in which it was found. Any damage to equipment, furniture or the building will be charged to the hirer. Spaces must be vacated promptly at the end of the hired period. Noise levels must not disturb other users of the building.

4. Capacity & Safety

Hirers must not exceed the stated capacity of the space. All hirers are responsible for ensuring the safety of their participants and must comply with fire safety procedures displayed in each space.

5. Equipment

Basic technical equipment is included where indicated. Additional technical support must be arranged in advance and may incur additional charges.

6. Catering

Hirers may bring their own refreshments but must remove all food waste at the end of the hire period. Hot food preparation is not permitted unless the kitchen has been booked separately.

7. Liability

Toynbee Studios / Artsadmin accepts no liability for loss or damage to property belonging to the hirer or their guests. Hirers are strongly advised to arrange their own public liability insurance.

8. Data Protection

Your personal data will be held in accordance with our Privacy Policy and used solely to administer your booking. We will not share your data with third parties except as required by law.
